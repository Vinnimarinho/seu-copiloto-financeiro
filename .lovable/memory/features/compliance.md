## CVM Compliance

The Lucius app is NOT registered with CVM, ANBIMA, or APIMEC. It operates as an **educational analysis tool**, not as:
- Consultoria de valores mobiliários (Res. CVM 19/2021)
- Analista de valores mobiliários (Res. CVM 20/2021)
- Assessor de investimento (Res. CVM 178/2023)

### Required disclaimers (every analysis/recommendation page):
- "Não constitui consultoria, recomendação de investimento ou oferta de valores mobiliários"
- "Rentabilidade passada não é garantia de resultados futuros"
- "A decisão de investimento é de responsabilidade exclusiva do usuário"

### Forbidden:
- Never use "recomendação de investimento" without "educativa/assistida" qualifier
- Never promise returns or guaranteed profitability
- Never execute or intermediate buy/sell orders
- Never favor specific brokers, funds, or institutions

## LGPD Compliance

- Cookie consent banner on first visit (CookieConsent component)
- Full Art. 18 rights listed in Privacy page
- Legal bases (Art. 7) documented
- DPO contact: privacidade@lucius.app
- Data deletion within 15 business days
- ANPD complaint reference
- No data used for AI training or sold to third parties

## Components
- `RegulatoryDisclaimer` — full or compact disclaimer for internal pages
- `CookieConsent` — LGPD cookie consent banner
- Footer in AppSidebar with compact regulatory notice
